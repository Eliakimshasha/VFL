Program Name: Empowering the society

Region Undertaking the Program: Dar es Salaam, Tanzania

Project Duration: January 2024 - December 2026

Coverage: National Level

This program is dedicated to empowering young individuals with the necessary skills, knowledge, and resources to create sustainable livelihoods. The initiative focuses on mentorship, leadership training, and digital innovation, ensuring that the youth are well-equipped to contribute meaningfully to society.

Through partnerships with local organizations and international stakeholders, the program aims to bridge the gap between education and employment by offering vocational training, career guidance, and entrepreneurship development programs.
